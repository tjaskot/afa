PROBLEM STATEMENT

Software development deficiencies in the DOD drive cost overruns and delay in capability delivery. NISEC does not have a reliable analytic method for projecting risk of delays or cost overruns. This project aims to provide an analytic framework and initial capability product to fill this capability gap.
